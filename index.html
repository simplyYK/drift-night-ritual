§ 08 — Honest Answers
Questions

Honest answers.

Transparent on dosage, sourcing, and claims. If we don't have a published clinical reference for it, it isn't in the formula.

Is DRIFT a sleep aid?+
No. DRIFT is a dietary supplement designed to support a calming evening ritual. It is not a pharmaceutical and makes no medical claims. The ingredients are clinically dosed adaptogens that help calm the mind, relax the body, and support natural sleep onset.
Will I feel groggy in the morning like with melatonin?+
DRIFT contains a small amount of natural melatonin from tart cherry — a fraction of the dose in commercial gummies. Most customers report waking sharper, not foggier. We deliberately avoided synthetic high-dose melatonin for this reason.
How quickly will I notice a difference?+
Adaptogens build effect over time. Most subscribers report meaningful changes in sleep onset and quality within 7–14 nights. We recommend tracking with an Oura ring, Apple Watch, or WHOOP for objective feedback.
What does it taste like?+
Honey Lavender is delicately floral with a soft sweetness. Vanilla Dusk is warm, lightly toasted, and creamy. Both are designed to taste like something between a tea and a latte — not medicinal, never bitter.
Can I skip, pause, or cancel my subscription?+
Always. Manage everything from your account dashboard — skip a delivery, pause for a month, or cancel in two clicks. No phone calls, no retention dark patterns.
Where do you source ingredients?+
Our ashwagandha is KSM-66® from Ixoreal Biomed in India. Reishi is dual-extracted from Real Mushrooms in the US. Magnesium glycinate is from Albion Minerals. Every batch ships with a third-party Certificate of Analysis available on our site.
Is it safe with my medications?+
As with any supplement, please consult your physician — particularly if you take SSRIs, blood thinners, thyroid medication, or are pregnant or nursing. We publish full ingredient lists with no proprietary blend obfuscation so you and your doctor have everything you need.
